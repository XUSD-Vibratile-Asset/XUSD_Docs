## Stake LUSD

1. **Buy LUSD**: Make sure that you have enough LUSD to stake to recieve the XUSD token.
2. **Approve contract**: Approve the staking contract to use the LUSD for deposits in the staking contract.
3. **Deposite LUSD**: Deposite your LUSD to start generating XUSD rewards.
4. **Earn**: Watch the XUSD grow!


## Withdraw LUSD

When the genesis phase is over, withdraw your tokens and make lp.

1. **Un-stake your tokens**: Withdraw the tokens from the contract and into your wallet.
2. **Create Liquidity**: Create liquidity with the aquired XUSD and a token of choice to make liquidity with fo the next farms.
3. **Deposit into Farm**: Approve the contract to use the LP token. Deposit into contract and recieve OneSwap rewards. 
4. **Earn**
