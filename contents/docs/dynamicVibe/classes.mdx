**Definition:**
Vibe classes are distinct categories of vibes determined by the community, each with specific rules and basis points. These classes help tailor the vibratile credits to various behaviors and transaction types within the Vibratile token ecosystem.

## Community-Determined Vibe Classes
**Community Involvement:**
The community plays a crucial role in defining and approving vibe classes. This decentralized approach ensures that the vibratile credit system aligns with the collective interests and goals of the community.

### Active Vibe Classes:
A publicly accessible page will display a running list of all currently active vibes. This transparency allows users to understand how their viberatile credits are being calculated and observe the impact on their positions.
