The LitPass is your very own passport and identification for the higher levels of this protocol. Those who own one will differentiate themselves from the other general users of the token. 

 - **Seigniorage**: Once over peg, inflation will take place to bring the token price back down to where it must be. This is called seigniorage and they are distributed to the LitPass holders purportional to the amount of XUSD the individual had burned. 
 
 - **Voting Power**: You must own one to participate in the Community Accountability Governance.

 - **User Rankings**: Users social status and access key to the various parts. 

 - **Secure Protocol Communication**: Encrypted protocol to user communication for importaint votes or community meddages.
