## Become part of the team

If you would like to contribute and play a vital role within the community of XUSD, you will be able to. Soon, there will be an application form for Administrator positions within the community.


## What do you get in return? 

The rewards will be discussed individually. There will be different ways to be rewarded for your contributions.
**It will be either in the form of:** Good Vibes such as being flagged as a Good Actor where you can accrue +5 Good Vibes every transaction, and/or XUSD tokens.
