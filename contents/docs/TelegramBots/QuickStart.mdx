# 🚀 Quick Start Guide to XUSD

This guide will help you get started with the necessary steps and materials to engage with **PulseChain** and effectively utilize **Vibratile Tokens** such as **XUSD**.

---

### 🌐 Getting Started with PulseChain

To connect to the PulseChain network, check out the detailed guide here: [Get Started with PulseChain](PulseChain).

---

## 💼 Stake Assigned Tokens for Genesis Phase Pools 

To participate in the Genesis phase and earn XUSD rewards, follow these steps:

1. **Choose an Assigned Token**  
   Select from one of the following tokens:  
   **PLS, PLSX, INC, eHEX, pHEX, pDAI, Atropa, 1SWAP, LOVE.IO, BFF, AFFECTION™, NBA™, Teddybear㉾, A1A, Finvesta, FKO, FRENS, DEX, LEMON, SIMP.**

2. **Buy the Token**  
   Ensure you have enough of your selected token to stake and receive staking rewards in XUSD.

3. **Approve the Contract**  
   Approve the staking contract to utilize your token for deposits.

4. **Stake the Token**  
   Stake your assigned token in the pool to start earning **XUSD rewards**.

5. **Earn**  
   Watch your XUSD grow as rewards accumulate over time!

---

## 💸 Withdrawing Staked Tokens

Once the Genesis phase concludes, you can withdraw your staked tokens and begin creating liquidity. There is **no time-lock** for withdrawals.

1. **Un-stake Your Tokens**  
   Withdraw your tokens from the contract back into your wallet.

2. **Create Liquidity**  
   Use the acquired XUSD and another token of your choice to create liquidity for the next farms.

3. **Stake Liquidity in Farms**  
   Approve the contract to use your **LP tokens**, stake them into the farm, and receive **OneSwap rewards**.

4. **Earn More**  
   Continue to earn rewards by providing liquidity and staking your LP tokens.

You will be able to trade XUSD on various **PulseChain DEXes** such as **PulseX V2**, **Piteas**, and others.
