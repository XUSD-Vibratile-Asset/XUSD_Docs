Protocol Launch
## Genesis 
Duration: 2 weeks

Genesis refers to the initial phase of the protocol. During this period, holders of specific tokens referenced in the Genesis page are able to participate. This is the first opportunity to attain XUSD tokens.
There will be a total of 40,000,000 XUSD tokens up for grabs during the Genesis phase.


## Bootstrapping
Duration: 1 year

Liquidity token farming the OneSwap token. 

## Operational

Duration: Forever

At this stage, we will hand over the control of the XUSD Vibratile Asset protocol to a DAO (decentralized autonomous organization) or another community.
